What We Offer

Our Dental
Services

Comprehensive care for every need — from routine checkups to full smile makeovers — all under one roof in Guwahati.

General Dentistry

The foundation of great oral health. Our preventive and restorative care keeps your teeth strong year-round.

  • Comprehensive oral examinations
  • Professional cleaning & scaling
  • Composite & amalgam fillings
  • Gum disease treatment
  • Tooth extractions

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening that delivers safe, dramatic results — brighter by up to 8 shades in a single visit.

  • In-office laser whitening
  • Take-home custom trays
  • Shade analysis & consultation
  • Sensitivity management
  • Long-term maintenance advice

Orthodontics

Straighten your teeth and correct bite problems with modern solutions tailored to your lifestyle.

  • Metal & ceramic braces
  • Clear aligners
  • Retainers & post-treatment care
  • Early intervention for children
  • Adult orthodontics

Root Canal Therapy

Modern, painless root canal treatments using the latest endodontic techniques to save your natural tooth.

  • Single-visit RCT available
  • Laser-assisted treatment
  • Digital apex locator
  • Rotary endodontics
  • Post-treatment crown placement

Dental Implants

The gold standard for tooth replacement — permanent, natural-looking results that last a lifetime.

  • Single tooth implants
  • Implant-supported bridges
  • Full-arch restoration (All-on-4)
  • Bone grafting when required
  • 3D implant planning

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care that builds positive associations with oral health from day one.

  • First dental visit (age 1+)
  • Fluoride treatments & sealants
  • Space maintainers
  • Painless extractions for children
  • Habit-breaking appliances

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with precision cosmetic procedures that enhance both aesthetics and confidence.

  • Porcelain veneers
  • Dental bonding
  • Full smile makeovers
  • Gum contouring
  • Tooth reshaping

Oral Surgery

Expert surgical care for complex dental conditions by our experienced oral and maxillofacial surgeon.

  • Wisdom tooth removal
  • Impacted tooth surgery
  • Cyst & tumour removal
  • Corrective jaw surgery
  • Oral biopsy
