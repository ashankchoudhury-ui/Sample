What We Offer

Our Dental Services

Comprehensive care under one roof — from routine checkups to advanced restorative and cosmetic procedures.

🦷

General Dentistry

The foundation of good oral health. We provide complete preventive and restorative dental care for your whole family.

  • Comprehensive oral examinations
  • Professional cleaning & scaling
  • Composite & amalgam fillings
  • Gum disease treatment
  • Tooth extractions
✨

Teeth Whitening

Professional whitening that delivers safe, dramatic results — brighter by up to 8 shades in a single visit.

  • In-office laser whitening
  • Take-home custom tray whitening
  • Shade analysis & consultation
  • Sensitivity management
  • Maintenance advice
😁

Orthodontics

Straighten your teeth and correct bite problems with modern orthodontic solutions tailored to your lifestyle.

  • Metal & ceramic braces
  • Clear aligners (Invisalign-style)
  • Retainers & post-treatment care
  • Early intervention for children
  • Adult orthodontics
🔬

Root Canal Therapy

Modern, painless root canal treatments using the latest endodontic techniques to save your natural tooth.

  • Single-visit RCT available
  • Laser-assisted treatment
  • Digital apex locator
  • Rotary endodontics
  • Post-treatment crown placement
🏥

Dental Implants

The gold standard for tooth replacement. Permanent, natural-looking implants that restore your smile completely.

  • Single tooth implants
  • Implant-supported bridges
  • Full-arch restoration (All-on-4)
  • Bone grafting when required
  • 3D implant planning
👶

Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle, child-friendly dental care designed to build positive associations with oral health from an early age.

  • First dental visit (age 1)
  • Fluoride treatments & sealants
  • Space maintainers
  • Painless extractions for children
  • Habit-breaking appliances
💎

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with precision cosmetic procedures that enhance both aesthetics and function.

  • Porcelain veneers
  • Dental bonding
  • Smile makeovers
  • Gum contouring
  • Tooth reshaping
🛡️

Oral Surgery

Expert surgical care for complex dental conditions performed by our experienced oral and maxillofacial surgeon.

  • Wisdom tooth removal
  • Impacted tooth surgery
  • Cyst & tumour removal
  • Jaw surgery
  • Oral biopsy
