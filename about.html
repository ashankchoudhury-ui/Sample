About
Sample Clinic

Trusted by Guwahati families since 2010 — delivering exceptional dental care with compassion, precision, and modern technology.

Founded 2010

A Clinic Built on
Trust & Excellence

Sample Clinic was founded by Dr. Ashank Choudhury with a clear vision — to bring world-class dental care to Guwahati in an environment that is warm, modern, and completely patient-focused.

What started as a modest practice has grown into a full multispeciality dental centre with 10+ specialists covering every aspect of oral health — from a child's first visit to complex full-mouth restorations.

We believe dentistry should never be feared. Our team goes above and beyond so every patient feels heard, comfortable, and confident at every single visit.
