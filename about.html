Our Story

About Sample Clinic

Trusted by Guwahati families since 2010 — delivering exceptional dental care with compassion, precision, and modern technology.

A Clinic Built on
Trust & Excellence

Sample Clinic was founded in 2010 by Dr. Ashank Choudhury with a clear vision — to bring world-class dental care to Guwahati in an environment that is warm, modern, and completely patient-focused.

What started as a modest clinic has grown into a multispeciality dental centre with a team of 10+ specialists covering every aspect of oral health. We have treated over 500 patients and counting, from toddlers visiting for their first checkup to seniors getting full smile restorations.

We believe that dentistry should never be feared. Our team goes above and beyond to ensure every patient feels heard, comfortable, and confident — from the moment they book an appointment to their post-treatment follow-up.
